Zizzz

Cooperation: Kamila Waszkowiak

Year: 2016

Conceptual project

Sleeping - Non Architectural Competitions

We present you Zizzz - hotel on demand. Click a button on your phone and the nearest driverless mobile bed will find you. It is small (what else would you need?), cheap (because you pay only for time spent in it), really quick (the firstnearest room would appear as soon as possible) and easy to order (via comfortable phone app).
