Prowizorka (Quick Fix)

Cooperation: Kamil Banach, Jakub Kowalski, Rafał Łasocha

Year: 2016

Mobile application

Hack4Culture 2016 - Microsoft Prize

Mobile application intended to promote street artists and informal culture of any kind. It can track locations of your favourite artists and art pieces in the neighbourhood and allows to donate them some money via PayPal.
