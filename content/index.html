Lights and shadows

An installation for the children's daycare centre in Warsaw. The intervention of „lighting up” the fence and elevations with small trembling mirrors makes the place more attractive both for children and adults.

Science and Technology Park in Opole

An urban and architectural concept of a research centre for new branches of industry. It consist of laboratories, office buildings and production halls connected with a common passage.

WUT Pavilion

An exhibition pavilion built for the 70th anniversary of Wrocław University of Technology. Wooden structure filled with cardboard tubes creates a unique space to present the historical timeline of the university.

The Gecko

A prototype of a digital fabricated facade system. It is based on a principle of connecting form and contraform by using scanning and 3d printing. Revarded with the main prize of the international workshops InDeSem 2015 re.craft in Delft.

Idea Box

An interior design for an apartment in Bratislava dedicated for a young, active couple. It has an open plan with a core storage box separting bedroom space from other zones.

Adapter

An urban project for the downtown of Bratislava. A combination of permament and temporary interventions in the urban fabric raises the standard of living in the neighbourhood and at the same time prevents it from the negative influance of gentrification.

Kindergarten in Brzeg

A conceptual project of a sustainable kindergarten consists of seven free-standing units connected with each other. By using glazed wooden frame structure the good quality of lighting and ventilation is provided.

ACH! Student Media Center

A restoration project for a modernistic auditorium building in Wrocław. The concept includes creating a cultural center combined with a fully-equipped recording studio and a cafe. Functional changes cause no harm for the preserved form of the building.
